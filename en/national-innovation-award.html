National Innovation Award

A MEI initiative, co-organized by CNI and Sebrae and promoted by FINEP, the National Innovation Award recognizes the best practices in innovation of industrial companies, small businesses from all sectors and innovation ecosystems throughout the country.

In this edition, one of the novelties is the category for Innovation Ecosystems, recognizing local cooperation networks that integrate triple helix organizations and that have innovation as a link.

Winners will be announced on March 8, 2022, the night before the 9th Brazilian Industry Innovation Summit. The award includes participation in two immersions in innovation ecosystems, one national and the other international, as well as places in the Master in Business Innovation (MBI) course of the National Service for Industrial Learning (SENAI).
