The Summit

Co-organized by the Brazilian National Confederation of Industry (CNI) and the Brazilian Micro and Small Business Support Service (Sebrae) since 2013, the largest innovation event in Latin America reaches its 9th edition in a hybrid format.Co-organized by the Brazilian National Confederation of Industry (CNI) and the Brazilian Micro and Small Business Support Service (Sebrae) since 2013, the largest innovation event in Latin America reaches its 9th edition in a hybrid format.

The Brazilian Industry Innovation Summit brings together decision makers from companies of all sizes, government representatives, academia and science and technology institutions to think about and debate innovation as the main strategy for growth and competitiveness.

About MEI

The Entrepreneurial Mobilization for Innovation (MEI) is the main space for public-private dialogue on CT& I policy and initiatives in Brazil.

It has the participation of over 400 CEOs froom the biggest companies in the country.

During the event, companies are called upon to learn and invest in market that will shape the future, led by the most innovative players.
